# AI Consulting Labs

A hands-on curriculum for becoming an AI Strategy, Governance and Implementation Consultant - from foundations through building, deploying, securing, and governing AI systems, to executive consulting.

## What's covered

- **Phase 0 - Orientation** - the AI profession and a working development environment.
- **Tier 1 - Python and software foundations** - Python, software engineering, SQL, APIs.
- **Tier 2 - Mathematics and statistics for AI** - algebra, linear algebra, probability, statistics, calculus intuition.
- **Tier 3 - Data analysis and data engineering** - NumPy, pandas, visualization, data quality, pipelines.
- **Tier 4 - Classical machine learning** - supervised and unsupervised learning, feature engineering, evaluation.
- **Tier 5 - Deep learning and NLP** - neural networks, PyTorch, natural-language processing, transformers.
- **Tier 6 - Generative AI and large language models** - LLM foundations, prompt design, model providers, APIs.
- **Tier 7 - Retrieval-Augmented Generation** - embeddings, vector databases, document processing, evaluation.
- **Tier 8 - AI agents, tools and MCP** - agent design, tool calling, Model Context Protocol, agent safety.
- **Tier 9 - AI application engineering** - backend, frontend, enterprise integration, architecture patterns.
- **Tier 10 - MLOps and LLMOps** - lifecycle, tooling, monitoring, testing, model drift.
- **Tier 11 - AI security** - application security, AI-specific threats, threat modeling, red teaming.
- **Tier 12 - Responsible AI and governance** - responsible-AI principles, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, impact assessments, policy.
- **Tier 13 - Cloud AI platforms** - AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud AI services.
- **Tier 14 - AI business strategy and product management** - opportunity finding, business cases, product management.
- **Tier 15 - AI consulting** - discovery, readiness assessment, deliverables, executive communication.
- **Tier 16 - Industry specialization** - public sector, financial services, healthcare, education.
- **Tier 17 - Research, publishing and thought leadership**.
- **Tier 18 - Capstone** - a complete, secure, governed AI adoption engagement.

Each unit includes concept references, step-by-step build guides, reinforcement exercises, troubleshooting scenarios, and interview preparation.
